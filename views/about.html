About

Everything to know about me, the developer


Hi, I am Marcus Brown, a student currently studying at Seneca Polytechnic for Computer Programming, though I have previously completed a 4 year Bachelor of Commerce majoring in Marketing from Toronto Metropolitan Univeristy.

Despite my degree from TMU which would give me many job prospects, I decided to pursue Computer Programming because I knew I would regret it if I didn't pursue something which I am passionate about.

I began learning to code in the summer before my first semester at Seneca. At first, I created simple projects like to-do lists and static page websites until I eventually learned about React.js. In order to learn React I decided it was time to start a bigger project. This is when I created my Training-Log App which made me struggle and learn a ton. My App enables users to track their workout without overcomplicating things as most of the currently available apps do. The App also supports exporting files to a csv for backup. Once I finish with all the features of this App I plan on creating a Library App for me and my friends to use.

As for hobbies and interests of mine aside from coding, you may already have guessed, but I am very passionate about Powerlifting, I love to read every day, occasionally I will cook an extravagent meal, and of course having a good time with friends.
